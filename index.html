Portfolio

StrikeDistance

StrikeDistance was the first app I developed.  It is a calculator that determines the distance between you and a lightning strike, given the current temperature and the time between the flash and boom.

StrikeDistance was originally a Windows Phone app written in Visual Basic. Although that app has been retired, I recreated it as a web app written in TypeScript/HTML/CSS.

Try it online
Merge

The Merge app is designed to keep the students of Central Bible Church (formerly Pantego Bible Church) engaged through upcoming events, Merge groups, announcements, and more.  The app also includes a leader area with aditional materials and an attendance tracker.

This project is composed of an Android app written in C# with Xamarin, an iOS app also written in C# with Xamarin, and a Windows-based CMS app written in C# with WPF. All apps take advantage of Firebase features including Realtime Database.

Authentic City Church

Church isn't a once-a-week event.  That's why the Authentic City Church app is designed to keep people engaged, involved, and updated throughout the week with everything happening at Authentic.

This project is composed of an Android app written in Kotlin, an iOS app written in Swift, and a web-based CMS written in JSX/HTML/CSS with NodeJS and React. All apps take advantage of Firebase features including Realtime Database and Crashlytics.
